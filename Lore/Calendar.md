# **The Harptos Calendar**

The Harptos calendar has:

- **12 months**, each with **30 days**
- **5 festival days** that exist _outside_ the months
- **A leap day** every 4 years

##  **Festival Days Explained**

These days don’t belong to any month:

- **Midwinter** – A day of reflection and noble gatherings
- **Greengrass** – Spring celebration of flowers and renewal
- **Midsummer** – Joyous summer festival
- **Shieldmeet** – Occurs every 4 years; day of treaties, oaths, and fairs
- **Highharvestide** – Autumn harvest festival
- **Feast of the Moon** – Honoring ancestors and the dead
