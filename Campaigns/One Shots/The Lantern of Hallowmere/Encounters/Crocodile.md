## **Old Snaggletooth (Scarred Crocodile)**
*Large beast, unaligned*

**Armor Class:** 13 (natural armor)  
**Hit Points:** 26 (4d10 + 4)  
**Speed:** 20 ft., swim 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 15 (+2) | 10 (+0) | 13 (+1) | 2 (-4) | 10 (+0) | 5 (-3) |

**Skills:** Stealth +4  
**Senses:** Passive Perception 10  
**Challenge:** 1 (200 XP)

---

### **Traits**
**False Appearance** 
While Snaggletooth remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from a mossy log.  

**Legendary Resistance (1/Day)** 
If Snaggletooth fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead. (Use this to keep the fight from ending in one turn).

### **Actions**
**Bite** 
*Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 7 (1d10 + 2) piercing damage. The target is **grappled** (escape DC 12). Until this grapple ends, the target is **restrained**.

### **Legendary Actions**
Old Snaggletooth can take 1 legendary action at the end of the player's turn:

* **Tail Swipe.** Snaggletooth swings its tail. The player must succeed on a **DC 12 Dexterity saving throw** or be knocked prone.
* **Thrash.** If Snaggletooth has a player grappled, it deals 3 (1d6) bludgeoning damage as it shakes them.
* **Slip Away.** Snaggletooth moves up to half its swim speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
